Privacy Policy

FE Global Education

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal, academic, and identification information including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and passport details
  • Academic records and test scores
  • Application and visa-related documents

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide consultation, guidance, and application services
  • Communicate important updates, notifications, and service-related information
  • Share necessary details with universities, partners, and immigration authorities with consent

3. Data Protection

FE Global Education uses appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Partner universities and educational institutions

  • Legal or regulatory bodies when required by law

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and enhance website functionality. Users may manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

6. User Consent

By accessing our website or using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

FE Global Education reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
